About Us

25 Years of Authentic Indian Catering

Welcome to Malkit's Sweets and Catering! With 25 years of experience in the catering industry, we are proud to serve the rich and diverse flavors of traditional Indian cuisine to customers across California and beyond. Our passion for culinary excellence is reflected in our wide range of catering services, including wedding catering, corporate events, and private parties. Our menu features a variety of authentic Indian dishes and sweets, from savory samosas and aromatic tandoori chicken to decadent gulab jamuns and melt-in-your-mouth ladoos. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and unforgettable culinary experiences. Let us make your special occasions truly memorable with our exquisite Indian catering and sweets.

Authentic Indian Sweets in California

At Malkit's Sweets and Catering, we take pride in offering traditional, handmade Indian sweets that bring the rich flavors and cultural heritage of India to California. Each sweet is crafted with love and the finest ingredients to ensure authenticity and quality.

From melt-in-your-mouth ladoos to decadent Jalebi, Gulab jamuns, our sweets are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're celebrating a special event or simply indulging in a sweet treat, our handmade sweets promise to delight your taste buds and leave you wanting more.
