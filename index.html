Welcome to Malkit Sweets & Catering

Welcome to Malkit Sweets and Catering Services, your premier destination for authentic Indian cuisine on the West Coast. With a passion for culinary excellence and a dedication to quality, our experienced master chefs bring the rich and diverse flavors of India to your table. From the spicy delights of Punjab to the aromatic dishes of South India, we offer a wide array of mouthwatering options that cater to all tastes and preferences. Our sweets are made with the finest ingredients, ensuring a taste that transports you straight to the heart of India. Our commitment to using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking techniques guarantees that every sweet is a celebration of Indian heritage and hospitality. Trust us to make your next event truly special with our exquisite menu, delectable sweets, and exceptional service.

Wedding Catering

Celebrate your special day with our exquisite wedding catering . Our menu features a variety of traditional Indian dishes, ensuring a memorable culinary experience for you and your guests.

Party Catering

Make your parties unforgettable with our customized party catering services. From birthdays to anniversaries, we offer a wide range of Indian delicacies to delight your guests.

Catering On Demand

Need catering on short notice? Our catering on demand services are designed to provide you with delicious Indian cuisine whenever you need it, ensuring your event runs smoothly.
